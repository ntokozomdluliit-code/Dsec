## 🚀 Overview

DSEC is a South African technology group building decentralized, secure, and locally-relevant IT solutions. We serve small businesses, communities, and individuals who are tired of expensive foreign cloud services and unreliable centralized systems.

**Our mission:** Technology on your terms. No single point of failure. No foreign cloud dependency.
